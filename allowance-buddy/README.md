## 🐾 Allowance Buddy — AI Financial Literacy Robot Dog
Python | MediaPipe | YOLOv8 | React Native | ElevenLabs | Supabase | Raspberry Pi

AI-powered robotic dog that teaches children aged 7+ financial literacy through real-time computer vision, gamified rewards, and voice interaction.

**My Key Contributions:**
- Led pose data collection and manufacturing across 20+ pose classes, building the MediaPipe pipeline to capture and save normalized body landmark coordinates per person per pose
- Trained a deep neural network on 10,000+ landmark data points to classify real-time child actions (sweeping, wiping table, reading, carrying laundry, and more)
- Designed and developed the iOS app UI including the Kids Portal (bank balance, chores checklist, day streak) and contributed to Parent and Teacher portal interfaces

**Traction:** Piloted with 20+ children at Little Tokyo Library, LA. Demoed at MAIA Demo Day in front of guests from Microsoft, Google, and OpenAI.
